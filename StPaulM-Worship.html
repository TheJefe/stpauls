St. Paul's TEC worships using the Episcopal Church's Book of Common Prayer.
 The services are Sunday morning at 10:30 a.m. Coffee follows the service.
  The service is a Holy Eucharist. The Episcopal Church welcomes all baptized Christians to take communion with us. 
Others are welcome to receive a blessing, or simply participate in the prayers.
